Interests

Garrett is a self-published sci-fi / fantasy author in need of a real job. As such, he is now drinking deeply from the DevMountain firehose, and only occasionally thowing up everything he thought he'd learned.

Skills

He is a talented Electronics Technician with over 10 years of experience; strong integrity, excellent interpersonal and leadership skills; and a track record of making complex procedures easy to follow and implement. He has done award-winning work on short- and long-term projects, and has a reputation for high-quality assembly work and equipment design.

Goals, hopes, and dreams

He hopes to one day soon be working from home, so that he can move his family somewhere between South Jordan (where his kids' dance classes are) and Provo (where his wife hopes to attend BYU) regardless of where his employer is actually located.

Back on the self-published author note, though, he's the proud father of such classics you've never heard of as:

  • The Formula, and
  • Special Developments
...as well as of such yet-to-be-published works (and works-in-progress) as:
  • Empire,
  • Aftershocks,
  • Leech,
  • Parallel, and
  • Entanglement
Therefore, he also hopes to make enough money coding to eventually retire while he still has an imagination (and can still see the keyboard), so that he may yet bring these and other beautiful children into the world.
